Expert Septic System Repair in Lafayette

When your septic system breaks down, you need a team that can diagnose the problem fast and get it fixed right the first time. Bayou Teche Septic provides professional septic repair for residential and commercial properties throughout Lafayette, LA and surrounding communities. From minor component replacements to major system overhauls, we handle every repair with the same commitment to quality workmanship and honest pricing.

Common Septic Repairs We Handle

  • Broken or deteriorated baffles that allow solids into the drain field
  • Cracked or damaged septic tank walls and lids
  • Failed or malfunctioning effluent pumps and alarms
  • Collapsed or root-clogged inlet and outlet pipes
  • Distribution box repair or replacement
  • Tank riser and lid installation or replacement
  • Float switch and control panel repairs for aerobic systems

Warning Signs Your Septic System Needs Repair

Knowing when to call for repair can save you thousands of dollars and prevent a full system failure. Watch for these warning signs that indicate your septic system needs professional attention.

  • Sewage odors inside the home or near the tank and drain field area
  • Multiple slow drains or toilets that do not flush properly
  • Gurgling or bubbling sounds in your plumbing system
  • Wet, soggy areas or standing water over the septic tank or drain field
  • Unusually green or lush grass growing over the drain field
  • Sewage backup in basement drains, bathtubs, or toilets
  • Septic alarm sounding on an aerobic treatment system
