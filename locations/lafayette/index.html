Lafayette's Trusted Septic Professionals

Lafayette is the heart of Acadiana and the seat of Lafayette Parish, home to more than 120,000 residents who enjoy a unique blend of Cajun culture, thriving industry, and Southern charm. As the largest city in the region, Lafayette supports thousands of residential properties and businesses that depend on properly functioning septic systems. From the established neighborhoods near downtown along Johnston Street to the growing subdivisions off Ambassador Caffery Parkway and Verot School Road, septic tanks are a critical part of daily life for homeowners throughout the city.

One of the most significant challenges facing septic system owners in Lafayette is the region's heavy clay soils combined with a persistently high water table. South Louisiana's flat terrain and frequent heavy rains mean that the ground stays saturated for extended periods, placing extra strain on drain fields and making it harder for effluent to properly percolate. Older neighborhoods like the Oil Center, Bendel Gardens, and along Kaliste Saloom Road often have septic systems installed decades ago that require more frequent pumping and careful monitoring to prevent backups and groundwater contamination. Meanwhile, newer developments in the Broussard and Youngsville corridor are seeing fresh installations that must comply with current Louisiana Department of Health standards for tank capacity, setback distances, and soil suitability testing.

Our team is headquartered right here in Lafayette, giving us unmatched local knowledge and the ability to respond quickly when you need service. We understand Lafayette Parish permitting requirements, know which soil conditions are typical in each neighborhood, and have working relationships with local inspectors. Whether you need routine septic pumping every three to five years, a pre-sale inspection for a real estate transaction, emergency repair for a failing system, or a complete new installation on a property along the I-10 corridor, we bring the expertise and equipment to handle the job right the first time.

We also serve the surrounding communities of Broussard, Youngsville, Scott, Carencro, and Duson, all within Lafayette Parish. Our reach extends into neighboring parishes as well, including Breaux Bridge in St. Martin Parish, Crowley in Acadia Parish, Opelousas in St. Landry Parish, and New Iberia in Iberia Parish. No matter where your property is located in the greater Acadiana region, Bayou Teche Septic provides reliable, honest septic care backed by our commitment to upfront pricing and professional workmanship.
