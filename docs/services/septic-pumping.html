Professional Septic Tank Pumping

Your septic tank is one of the hardest-working components of your home, processing every drop of wastewater that leaves your sinks, showers, toilets, and appliances. Over time, solid waste accumulates at the bottom of the tank as sludge, while fats and oils float to the top forming a scum layer. Without regular pumping, these layers build up until they overwhelm the tank's capacity and begin flowing into your drain field, causing costly damage and potential health hazards.

For most Lafayette Parish households, septic pumping should happen every 3 to 5 years, though this depends on tank size, household size, and water usage. A family of four with a standard 1,000-gallon tank typically needs pumping closer to the 3-year mark, while smaller households with larger tanks may go the full 5 years between services. Our technicians assess your specific situation and recommend a maintenance schedule that keeps your system healthy without unnecessary expense.

Lafayette's clay-heavy soils and high water table create unique challenges for septic systems in our area. The slower percolation rates common throughout Lafayette Parish mean your drain field works harder than systems in sandier regions, making it even more important to keep your tank properly maintained. When sludge levels get too high, partially treated effluent enters the drain field and clogs the soil, leading to backups, soggy yards, and foul odors that no homeowner wants to deal with.

Septic pumping costs in the Lafayette area typically range from $250 to $450 for a standard residential tank. The exact price depends on tank size, accessibility, and how long it has been since the last pumping. We provide upfront pricing before any work begins, so you always know what to expect. Our team handles everything from locating and uncovering the tank to pumping, basic inspection, and proper sludge disposal at licensed facilities.

Our Septic Pumping Process

We follow a thorough, proven process for every septic pumping job to ensure your system gets the care it needs.

1

Schedule Service

Call us or fill out our form to book a convenient appointment. We offer flexible scheduling, including same-day availability for urgent situations.

2

Locate the Tank

Our technicians locate your septic tank using records, probing, or electronic locating equipment, then uncover the access lids for pumping.

3

Pump the Tank

Using our high-powered vacuum truck, we remove all sludge, scum, and liquid from the tank, ensuring a thorough cleanout every time.

4

Inspect Components

While the tank is open, we inspect the baffles, tank walls, and inlet/outlet pipes for cracks, deterioration, or other issues that need attention.

5

Report & Recommend

You receive a written report of the tank condition, sludge levels, and our recommendations for your next pumping interval or any needed repairs.
