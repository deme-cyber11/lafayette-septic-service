Septic Experts for Broussard's Growing Community

Broussard has experienced remarkable growth over the past two decades, transforming from a quiet farming community south of Lafayette into one of the most sought-after residential areas in Acadiana. Subdivisions like Copper Meadows, Sugar Mill Pond, and the developments along the Ambassador Caffery corridor have brought thousands of new homes to the area, and many of these properties rely on individual septic systems rather than municipal sewer connections. As Broussard continues to expand, the demand for professional septic services has never been higher.

The mix of new and established properties in Broussard creates unique septic service needs. Newer subdivisions typically feature modern aerobic treatment systems that require regular maintenance, inspection of spray heads, and periodic chlorine tablet replacement to keep them operating within Louisiana DHH compliance standards. Meanwhile, the older sections of Broussard along Main Street and the Highway 90 corridor have conventional gravity-fed septic systems that may be twenty or thirty years old. These mature systems often need more frequent pumping and careful inspection of baffles, distribution boxes, and drain field lines to ensure they are still functioning properly.

Located just a short drive south of our Lafayette base, Broussard is one of our fastest-response service areas. Whether you are a new homeowner who needs your first septic tank pumped, a real estate agent requiring a pre-sale septic inspection, or a long-time resident dealing with a drain field issue caused by the area's silty clay loam soils, our licensed team has the experience and equipment to handle the job right. We are familiar with Broussard's permitting requirements and work regularly with local officials to ensure every installation and repair meets code.
