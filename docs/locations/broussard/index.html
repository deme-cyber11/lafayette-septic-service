Reliable Septic Service for Broussard Homeowners

Broussard has transformed from a quiet farming community into one of the most sought-after suburbs in Lafayette Parish. Located just south of Lafayette along the US-90 corridor, this growing city has seen an explosion of new residential developments, commercial centers, and mixed-use projects over the past two decades. With that rapid growth has come a significant increase in the number of properties relying on individual septic systems, particularly in the newer subdivisions stretching along Ambassador Caffery Parkway and south toward Youngsville.

The soil conditions in Broussard present specific challenges for septic system owners. Much of the area sits on alluvial clay deposits left by the Vermilion River and its tributaries, which means the ground holds water rather than allowing it to drain freely. During Louisiana's rainy season, which can stretch from late spring through early fall, the water table in many Broussard neighborhoods rises close to the surface. This puts extra pressure on drain fields and can cause effluent to back up into yards or, worse, into homes. Older properties along Main Street and in the original town center may have aging concrete tanks that are more susceptible to cracking and root intrusion, while newer installations in developments like Copper Meadows and Sugar Mill Pond must meet current Louisiana Department of Health standards for setbacks, capacity, and soil testing.

Lafayette Septic Service understands Broussard's unique conditions because we work in the area regularly. Whether your home is in an established neighborhood near St. Cecilia or in one of the newer master-planned communities, we provide the full range of septic services: routine pumping every three to five years, pre-purchase inspections for real estate closings, emergency repair of failing systems, drain field restoration, new system installations, and commercial grease trap cleaning for the growing restaurant scene along the Ambassador corridor.

Our proximity to Broussard means faster response times for both scheduled service and emergencies. We also serve neighboring communities including Lafayette, Youngsville, Scott, Carencro, and Duson within Lafayette Parish, as well as Breaux Bridge, Crowley, Opelousas, and New Iberia in the surrounding parishes. Every job comes with upfront pricing, professional workmanship, and the peace of mind that comes from hiring a licensed and insured local team.
