Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Lafayette Septic Service ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website, lafayettesepticservice.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our contact form or reach out to us directly. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address or location
  • Details about the service you are requesting

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, financial account details, or payment card information through our website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • To provide accurate service quotes and estimates
  • To schedule and coordinate septic services
  • To follow up on completed services if needed
  • To improve our website and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services to operate effectively:

  • Web3Forms — We use Web3Forms to process contact form submissions. When you submit our contact form, your information is transmitted through Web3Forms' servers. You can review their privacy policy at web3forms.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website, including pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects anonymized data. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity — We use Microsoft Clarity to analyze user experience through session recordings and heatmaps. Clarity collects anonymized usage data to help us improve our website. You can learn more at Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies provided by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity. These cookies help us analyze website traffic and understand user behavior. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by adjusting your browser settings or using browser extensions

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Contact Us
